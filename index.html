ABOUT HSF

In the ashes a community emerged, a Smoking flog, a more based Smoking flog, a HSF. HSF has no dev. It is the community.

01. Create Wallet

Download Phantom or your wallet of choice from the app store or Google Play for free. Desktop users, download the Google Chrome extension by going to Phantom.

02. GET SOME SOL

Have SOL in your wallet to switch to $HSF. If you don’t have any SOL, you can buy directly on Phantom, transfer from another wallet, or buy on another exchange and send it to your wallet.

03. GO TO JUPITER

Connect to Jupiter. Connect your wallet in chrome. Paste the $HSF token address, select $HSF, and confirm. When Phantom prompts you for a wallet signature, sign.

04. SWAP FOR HSF

Switch SOL for $HSF. We have Zero taxes so you don’t need to worry about buying with a specific slippage.
